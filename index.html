Welcome to my website


My name is Andreas Fagerholm and I am a researcher in political science (comparative politics) at Åbo Akademi University (Faculty of Social Sciences, Business and Economics) in Vaasa, Finland.

My current research is focused on parties, legislatures and governments. I am also interested in political methodology (qualitative, quantitative and mixed methods) and in the history and philosophy of political science.

My hobbies include, in no particular order, travelling, hiking, groundhopping, cooking, literature, history, science, Stockholmiana, railways & trainspotting (esp. tram and rapid transit systems), and (the investigation of) the assassination of Olof Palme.



I am also obsessed with making lists, so here is one of my three most recently read books (excluding political science literature):

Den osannolika mördaren: Skandiamannen och mordet på Olof Palme (Offside Press, 2018)

Baude, Frank et al., eds:
Mordet på Palme och polisspåret (KPML(r), 1989)
